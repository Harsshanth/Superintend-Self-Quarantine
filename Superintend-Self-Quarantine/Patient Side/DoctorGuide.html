Do's

1. Do participate in “social distancing” to blunt the spread of the virus.
2. Do wear mask (N-95 advisable) at all times when you are not in home.
3. Do wash your hands frequently, especially before you eat, after you use the restroom, blow your nose or cough or before you touch your face,before you wear your mask.
4. Do wipe down all surfaces you come into contact with.
5. Do call ahead to the doctor/emergency room before you head there if you think you may have symptoms concerning for COVID-19.
6. Do protect your immune system by getting your vaccinations, eating a balanced diet, avoiding too much alcohol, getting enough sleep and maintaining a healthy weight.
7. Do self-quarantine yourself for 14 days if you come into contact with someone who tests positive for coronavirus.

Dont's

1. Firstly, Don’t panic.
2. Don’t touch your eyes, nose, mouth or face without washing your hands.
3. Don’t share utensils or drinking glasses with others, including family members.
4. Don’t shake hands or give hugs as greetings. Use an alternative greeting that maintains 6-8 feet of distance.
5. Don’t visit your older relatives or community members, as they are the highest risk group.
6. Don’t go to public places like gyms, theaters, restaurants.
